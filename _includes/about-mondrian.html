01 · Story

Research depth, product-minded implementation

Two threads run through my work: scientific modeling that earns trust through rigor, and product engineering that gets useful tools into people's hands.

Now · Engineering

Since April 2026, I have been an AI / Systems Engineer at Vectonomous Labs, working at the intersection of applied AI systems, backend infrastructure, data pipelines, and agentic engineering workflows.

In 2025, I founded Octidy, a Kotlin and Jetpack Compose Android app for household chore coordination. I owned the full path from product concept to Play Store testing: bidding logic, Firebase Auth, Firestore realtime sync, offline persistence, WorkManager deadlines, responsive UI, release management, Git workflows, and documentation.

Background · Research

I hold a PhD in Computational Neuroscience with 8+ years of experience working with ODE-based models, parameter estimation, sensitivity analysis, and profile likelihood analysis in MATLAB and Python. My thesis, A workflow for developing biochemical pathway models using ordinary differential equations, introduced an integrated FAIR-compliant software framework for biochemical pathway modeling.

Earlier, my MSc and research scholarship work focused on porphyrin nanomaterials via ionic self-assembly, combining wet-lab synthesis, characterization, and a publication in Tetrahedron.
